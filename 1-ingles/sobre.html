Who are we?:

Which prices there are for advertise my ad in the magazine?:

In the magazine the prices change by the size of an advertisement in a page, or if It's for the cover or the back-cover, and you pay by months. At the same price you have 3 languages to choose, and the advertisement will be published on our magazine, our social medias and our website
